Hospital Management

A compact Odoo app for patient intake and appointment scheduling

The module is designed as a lightweight hospital workflow that is easy to test, demo, and extend. It focuses on clean patient records, appointment tracking, and a practical backend user interface.

Features

  • Automatic patient and appointment references using sequences
  • Patient admission and discharge workflow
  • Appointment confirmation, completion, and cancellation flow
  • Age calculation, date validation, and quick statistics
  • List, form, search, and calendar views

Usage

Open the Hospital app, create a patient, then schedule one or more appointments from the patient form or the appointment menu.
